# TechTrack — quick context

## What it is

TechTrack is a web app for **theatre tech crew operations**: shows, directors **requesting tech help**, **posting applications** for students, **assigning** technicians, **email templates** and a **pending-email queue** for reminders, plus **student** and **resource** directories. Built with **React** and **Supabase**. The person behind it runs the whole workflow **solo**.

## What I’m looking for overall

**One home base** instead of juggling **Google Forms**, **Classroom links**, and **manual due-date tracking**. Directors should clearly see **their shows and status**; I should see **what to do next** without digging. **Automated nudges** (e.g. before tech week) with sensible wording—not retyping the same email every time.

When you help with the codebase, **improve what exists**—don’t replace the whole app unless there’s a strong reason. Small, practical steps.
